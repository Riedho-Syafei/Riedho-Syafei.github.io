Origin

Hello fellow readers, welcome to my private space, where I usually exist.

About my real life origin, I was actually quite late to the party. Riedho had been in the furry fandom for a few years, then he decided to create me.

As for my lore, here it is: I was born in a forest. As an adolescent maned wolf, my parents withdrew emotional and physical care after puberty, normalizing neglect saying things like "that's just how we maned wolves are," and my older brother ignored me too. I lived alone, became depressed, neglected bodily needs (e.g., not eating), and developed suicidal thoughts.

Then Riedho appeared, offered me a chosen role as a servant (which I initially questioned but consented to), brought me into a new family with Sirius, Draco, and Raqeem, and promised me that I would never be alone again — an experience that healed my abandonment wound and gave me a sense of belonging.
