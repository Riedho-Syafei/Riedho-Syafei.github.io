Origin

OH MY GOSH, HAII!! I'm Raqeem, nice to meet you, dear readers! So you want to know my origin, right? In real life, I was created when Riedho wants to create his first fursona. Riedho can't decide whether he wants a wolf or a husky fursona, so he created them both! You know who is the wolf, right? Correct! It is Sirius!

As for my lore, I think it's pretty dark... I have two canons, both are true at the same time!

📖 Canon 1 I was born in an animal adoption shelter. I was once adopted by a human. He was harsh and rude! So I ran away from home and became a stray dog. Life on the street was difficult! After a few years, Riedho found me. He offered me to become his pet. I agreed! He gave me safety and family!
📖 Canon 2 I was born in the Middle East. My country was at war with the West. I immigrated to a peaceful country in the South West. Both of my parents died during the immigration. I became a street child. Life on the street was difficult! After a few years, Riedho found me and offered me an adoption. I agreed! Now I have a dad! My dad gave me safety and family!
